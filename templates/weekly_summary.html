How it's calculated (click to expand)

Effort Factor (based on RIR — Reps In Reserve):

  • RIR 0–1 (near failure): 100%
  • RIR 2–3: 85%
  • RIR 4–5: 70%
  • RIR 6+: 55%
  • Not set: 100% (neutral default)

Rep Range Factor (based on average of min/max reps):

  • 1–5 reps: 85%
  • 6–20 reps (hypertrophy optimal): 100%
  • 21–30 reps: 85%
  • 31+ reps: 70%

Muscle Contribution Weights (Total mode):

  • Primary muscle: 100%
  • Secondary muscle: 50%
  • Tertiary muscle: 25%
  • Direct Only mode: only primary counted at 100%
