User Profile

About this page

Filling out this profile personalises the suggestions on the Plan page — Min Sets, Max Sets, Reps, RIR, RPE, and starting Weight for every exercise you add.

  • Demographics → seeds a cold-start Weight estimate (via bodyweight × gender / experience ratios) when no Reference Lift covers the muscle.
  • Reference Lifts → seeds the Weight suggestion on the Plan page, via the Epley 1RM formula and tier ratios.
  • Rep-Range Preferences → seeds Min Reps, Max Reps, RIR, and RPE on the Plan page, via Heavy / Moderate / Light presets per tier.

Every value is a starting suggestion — you can override it directly on the Plan page at any time.

How does this work?

Your reference lifts get converted into a working-weight suggestion in three steps:

  1. Estimate your 1RM from a recent hard set using the Epley formula: 1RM ≈ weight × (1 + reps / 30).
  2. Apply a tier ratio based on the target exercise:
    • Complex (compound barbell lifts) — ×1.00
    • Accessory (machines, dumbbell variants) — ×0.70
    • Isolated (single-joint isolation) — ×0.40
  3. Apply your rep-range preset for that tier:
    • Heavy — 4–6 reps, RIR 1, RPE 9 — ×0.85 of 1RM
    • Moderate — 6–8 reps, RIR 2, RPE 8 — ×0.77 of 1RM
    • Light — 10–15 reps, RIR 2, RPE 7.5 — ×0.65 of 1RM

Example. Barbell Back Squat @ 100 kg × 5 reps → Epley 1RM ≈ 117 kg → Leg Press suggestion (accessory tier × moderate preset): 117 × 0.70 × 0.77 ≈ 63 kg × 6–8 reps.

Reps and effort targets come from your Rep-Range Preferences below. Override anything you want directly on the Plan page.
