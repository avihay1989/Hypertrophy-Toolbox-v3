How to measure
  • Neck: below the larynx, tape sloping downward to the front; do not flare neck outward.
  • Waist (men): horizontal at the navel; do not pull the stomach inward.
  • Waist (women): at the smallest natural width.
  • Hip (women only): largest horizontal hip circumference.
